This project is a fork of ourongxing/newsnow, a Chinese real-time news aggregator, adapted for English-speaking audiences. All 40+ Chinese sources and UI labels were removed and replaced with 10 English content columns and 50 new sources spanning world news, tech, AI, finance, sports, science, entertainment, and podcasts. Sources include major outlets like CNN, BBC, Reuters, HackerNews, ESPN, and NASA, as well as YouTube podcast channels and Reddit communities.
